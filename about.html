Our Beginning
Our Origin Story

From a Factory Floor
to the World Stage

"We began with a single mission — to prove that premium sportswear and world-class logistics can originate from Pakistan."

Circinus Sports was established in Sialkot — a globally recognized hub for high-performance sports equipment and manufacturing excellence. What started as a focused OEM setup with a small team and a clear vision has evolved into a vertically integrated manufacturer and international trade partner serving clients across multiple continents.

Today, we specialize in custom gym wear, streetwear, sportswear, and performance apparel for international brands, fitness chains, and retail distributors. Our in-house logistics division manages everything from air and sea freight to customs clearance — ensuring a seamless journey from production to final delivery.

With thousands of successful shipments and a strong commitment to reliability, we operate with precision, speed, and a zero-compromise approach to quality — continuously pushing forward.
