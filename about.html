About Circinus Sports

Built for Performance,
Designed for the World

“From a small workshop in Sialkot to a global sportswear manufacturer — our journey is built on quality, trust, and consistency.”

Founded in 2014 in Sialkot, Pakistan — the heart of the global sports manufacturing industry — Circinus Sports started as a small OEM unit with a clear vision: to create premium-quality sportswear that meets international standards.

Over the years, we have grown into a fully integrated sportswear manufacturing and export company, specializing in gym wear, streetwear, boxing gear, and custom team uniforms. Today, we proudly serve clients in more than 90 countries.

What sets us apart is not just manufacturing — but complete supply chain solutions. From production to packaging, logistics, customs clearance, and global delivery, we handle everything end-to-end.

With over 5,000 successful shipments delivered worldwide, we continue to push forward with one goal: becoming a global leader in sportswear innovation.
