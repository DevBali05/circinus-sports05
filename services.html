Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before placing your first order with Circinus Sports. Still have questions? Reach us anytime.

Ask Us Directly
What is your minimum order quantity?
Our production is highly flexible and tailored to each client’s requirements. We support both small-batch production and large-scale manufacturing, depending on your product type, customization level, and business goals.
Do you offer samples?
We provide sample development to ensure your product meets exact specifications before full production. Sample timelines and terms are discussed based on the design and project scope.
How do I get a price quote?
Simply send us a JPG image or PDF of your required design along with the quantity, style, and fabric preference. We'll send back a detailed price breakdown within 24 hours via email or WhatsApp.
What printing and decoration methods do you offer?
We offer full dye sublimation, screen printing, embroidery, heat transfer (DTF/HTV), and rubber printing. Each method has different best-uses — we'll recommend the right one based on your design and fabric.
How long does bulk production take?
Standard bulk production is 4–6 weeks after sample approval. Rush orders can be arranged — contact us with your deadline and we'll do our best to accommodate. Rush lead times may affect pricing.
Which countries do you ship to?
We ship to 90+ countries worldwide. Popular destinations include the UK, USA, UAE, Australia, Germany, Canada, and across Europe and Asia. We handle all export documentation and customs paperwork from our end.
Can I use my own design / logo?
Absolutely. You can send us your own design files in any format (AI, PSD, PDF, JPG) and we'll produce exactly to your spec. If you don't have a design ready, our team can create one for you at no extra cost.
